Background

Prior to studying at Berkeley, I spent three years at Los Medanos College. Growing up with a professor as a dad, I've always planned on going to college, though I didn't have a clue what I wanted to be. I didn't discover my interest in astropysics until partway through my time at my local community college.

Family and Faith have always been very important to me. Childhood memories recall summers spent roadtripping across the country, spending time with friends and family along the way.

Hobbies

I try to maintain a balance between studying and socializing through my involvement in various clubs on campus.

Photography

My most consistent hobby, I enjoy capturing moments of joy with loved ones, cozy solitude, and (in true college spirit) the various ways I like to take my coffee.
